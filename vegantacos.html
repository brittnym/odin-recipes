Vegan Jackfruit Tacos

<

Description

If you're looking for a tasty vegan meal, jackfruit tacos are the way to go! Made with a "meaty" fruit that emmulates chicken in its texture, jackfruit is the perfect alternative for those who choose to steer clear of animal products.

This quick and easy recipe is sure to plwasw everyone at the dinner table!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Water
  • Taco seasoning
  • Taco shells
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add jackfruit; cook and stir for 2 to 3 minutes. Add 1 tablespoon water and taco seasoning; stir until well combined, about 1 minute. Add additional water 1 tablespoon at a time as needed.
  2. Place a small amount of jackfruit in each taco shell; add 2 tablespoons salsa.
